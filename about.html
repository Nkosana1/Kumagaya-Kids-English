About Our School

Our Mission

At Kumagaya Kids English, we are dedicated to providing a bright, friendly, and educational environment where children can learn English through fun and engaging experiences. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to learn English in a supportive, nurturing atmosphere that celebrates their unique learning journey.

Our Teaching Philosophy

We believe in playful learning that emphasizes child development and creates a trustworthy atmosphere where young minds can thrive. Our approach combines:

  • Play-Based Learning: Children learn best when they're having fun
  • Individual Attention: Small class sizes ensure every child gets personalized support
  • Cultural Awareness: We celebrate diversity and help children understand different cultures
  • Confidence Building: We create a safe space where mistakes are part of learning

Our Safe Learning Environment

Safety is our top priority. Our school facilities are designed with children in mind, featuring:

  • Bright, spacious classrooms with age-appropriate furniture
  • Secure entry systems and regular safety drills
  • Clean, well-maintained learning spaces
  • Emergency protocols and first-aid trained staff
