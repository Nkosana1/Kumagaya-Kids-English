Age Groups

Programs tailored to your child's developmental stage

Toddlers (Ages 2-3)

Program Focus: Introduction to English through play, songs, and sensory activities

Class Duration: 30 minutes, 2 times per week

Learning Activities:

  • Simple songs and nursery rhymes
  • Colorful flashcards and picture books
  • Movement and dance activities
  • Basic vocabulary (colors, numbers, animals)

Our toddler program introduces English in a gentle, playful way. Parents are welcome to join, creating a comfortable first learning experience.

Learn More

Preschool (Ages 4-5)

Program Focus: Building vocabulary and basic conversation skills through interactive play

Class Duration: 45 minutes, 2-3 times per week

Learning Activities:

  • Interactive games and storytelling
  • Simple conversation practice
  • Arts and crafts in English
  • Phonics introduction

Preschoolers learn through structured play, building confidence in speaking English while developing social skills and creativity.

Learn More

Lower Elementary (Ages 6-8)

Program Focus: Developing reading, writing, and conversation skills

Class Duration: 60 minutes, 2-3 times per week

Learning Activities:

  • Reading simple English books
  • Writing sentences and short stories
  • Grammar games and exercises
  • Group projects and presentations

Elementary students build comprehensive English skills through engaging activities that prepare them for academic success.

Learn More

Upper Elementary (Ages 9-12)

Program Focus: Advanced communication skills and academic English

Class Duration: 60-75 minutes, 2-3 times per week

Learning Activities:

  • Advanced reading comprehension
  • Essay writing and composition
  • Debate and discussion activities
  • Test preparation support

Older elementary students develop advanced English skills through challenging, age-appropriate activities that build confidence and academic readiness.
