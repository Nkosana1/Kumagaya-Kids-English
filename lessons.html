English Lessons

Discover our engaging lesson programs designed to make learning English fun and effective

Interactive Conversation Classes

Learning Objectives:

  • Build confidence in speaking English
  • Develop natural pronunciation and intonation
  • Practice everyday conversation skills
  • Learn through role-play and interactive games

Our conversation classes focus on practical English that children can use in real-life situations. Through games, role-play, and group activities, students practice speaking in a supportive environment.

Reading & Vocabulary Building

Learning Objectives:

  • Expand vocabulary through stories and activities
  • Develop reading comprehension skills
  • Learn phonics and word recognition

Writing & Grammar Skills

Learning Objectives:

Music & Song-Based Learning
